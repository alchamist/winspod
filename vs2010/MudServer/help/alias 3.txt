^YSyntax: ^NAlias <command 1>;<command 2>;<comm....
^YAliases: ^NNone

^PEffect:^N

Aliases can also contain random content, in the form of

{<choice 1>|<choice 2>|<choice 3>}

When the command is executed, one of the choices will be randomly selected and inserted into the output.


^RRestrictions:^N

Aliases can only contain system commands, not other aliases.
